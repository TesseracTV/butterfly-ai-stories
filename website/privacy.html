Privacy Policy

Butterfly AI Stories (“we”) respects your privacy.

Data we collect

We collect only what is needed to run the app: device identifiers for registration and API access, and the photos and story choices you use to generate stories. Story generation is processed on our servers.

How we use it

We use this data to provide story generation, manage your points and in‑app purchases, and improve the service. We do not sell your personal data.

Third parties

We use Apple (App Store, in‑app purchases) and other service providers necessary to operate the app. Their privacy policies apply to their handling of data.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact us at the support email listed on the main page or in the app.
